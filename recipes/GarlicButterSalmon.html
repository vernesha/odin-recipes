Garlic Butter Salmon w/Potatoes and Asparagus

Description

Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Baked Salmon with crispy potatoes, asparagus and a garlic
butter sauce with a touch of lemon. A complete meal on one tray using minimal
ingredients you already have in your kitchen! Full of flavour and so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Fingerling Potatoes
    • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cracked Black Pepper
  • 4 6-ounces Skinless Salmon Filets
  • 2 1/2 Garlic
  • 2 tablespoons Parsley
  • 1/3 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 cup Lemon Juice
  • 3 bunches Asparagus
  • 2 tablespoons Dry White Wine
  • 1 Lemon

Steps

  1. Bake your potatoes first as salmon needs less time in the oven. This way you're guaranteed the potatoes will be cooked through when it's all finished. I used fingerling potatoes in this recipe because we LOVE the flavour of them! You can also use Yukon golds or halved baby potatoes
  2. Once your potatoes are done, push them aside and pile on your asparagus and salmon pieces.
  3. Pour on all your flavours.
  4. Prepare an amazing garlic butter sauce with a hint of white wine and
    lemon juice.
  5. Preheat oven to 400 F, cook salmon for 8-10 minutes.

How to make garlic butter sauce

  1. Mince your garlic cloves.
  2. Rub the minced garlic all over salmon fillets.
  3. Combine lemon juice with melted butter and pour it all over your salmon and
    asparagus.
